2.1. Water State Above Sea Level

• The boiling point of water drops by about one degree celsius for every
  300 meters of altitude.

Write a C program that
    • Asks an input of 1 for Celsius and 2 for Fahrenheit and terminates the
      program if an incorrect input is provided.
    • Asks for and reads in a temperature value.
    • Asks for and reads in the altitude in meters.
    • Decides on the state of water at the given temperature at the given altitude.
    • Prints out whether water is liquid, solid, or gaseous at the given temperature
      at the given altitude.
